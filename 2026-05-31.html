The OBBBA's 2026 tax season provisions — a $40,000 SALT cap, permanent bonus depreciation, the new $6,000 senior deduction, Trump Accounts, expanded QSBS exclusions, and modified charitable deduction floors — represent the most significant structural change to the individual return since the TCJA, and they're all landing at once.
